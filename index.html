About Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born 5 February 1985, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards and holds numerous records.
